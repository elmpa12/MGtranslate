Help Center

How do I get started?

Click "Get Started", sign in with your Google account, then enter a meeting code to begin translating.

Which languages are supported?

We support English, Portuguese, Spanish, French, German, Italian, Japanese, Chinese, Korean, and many more.

Is my data secure?

Yes. Audio is processed in real-time and never stored. We follow strict privacy guidelines.

Do other participants need Transla.to?

No. Only the person wanting translations needs to use Transla.to. Other participants join normally.

Why do I need to authorize access?

Authorization allows Transla.to to access meeting audio through official platform APIs securely.
