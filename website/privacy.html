Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 1, 2024

Falcon Soft ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our services and products, including MGtranslate.

Information We Collect

We may collect information about you in various ways:

  • Personal Data: Name, email address, and contact information you provide when contacting us or using our services.
  • Usage Data: Information about how you interact with our services, including access times and pages viewed.
  • Audio Data: When using MGtranslate, audio streams are processed in real-time for translation purposes. Audio data is not stored permanently and is deleted immediately after processing.
  • Google Account Data: When you authenticate with Google services, we access only the data necessary for the service to function (e.g., Google Meet audio streams for translation).

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve our services
  • Process real-time audio translation
  • Respond to your inquiries and provide customer support
  • Send you technical notices and security alerts
  • Comply with legal obligations

Data Retention

We retain personal information only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this policy. Audio data processed for translation is not stored and is deleted immediately after the translation is complete.

Third-Party Services

Our services may integrate with third-party services including:

  • Google APIs: For Google Meet integration and authentication
  • OpenAI: For translation processing
  • Cloud Services: For hosting and infrastructure

These services have their own privacy policies governing the use of your information.

Google API Services User Data Policy

Our use of information received from Google APIs adheres to the Google API Services User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements.

Specifically:

  • We only request access to data necessary for providing our translation service
  • We do not use Google user data for advertising purposes
  • We do not sell Google user data to third parties
  • Audio data from Google Meet is processed in real-time and not stored

Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information, including:

  • Encryption of data in transit using TLS/SSL
  • Secure authentication mechanisms
  • Regular security assessments
  • Access controls and monitoring

Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights regarding your personal information, including:

  • Access to your personal data
  • Correction of inaccurate data
  • Deletion of your data
  • Data portability
  • Withdrawal of consent

Children's Privacy

Our services are not intended for individuals under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.
